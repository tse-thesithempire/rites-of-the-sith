RITES OF THE SITH

"The Force flows through ritual as blood flows through veins."

The Rite of Ascension

When an apprentice has proven their mastery, they undergo the Rite of Ascension—a private trial before the Dark Council wherein they must defeat a chosen adversary in single combat.

To the victor belongs the title of Sith Lord.

The Binding of the Apprentice

An apprentice is bound to their Master through a sacred ritual conducted in the Dark Temple. The apprentice swears absolute loyalty, and the Master accepts the burden of instruction.

Breaking the bond is the gravest of dishonors.

The Vigil of the Fallen

When a Sith falls, the Empire observes a vigil—a night of silence and meditation upon the impermanence of even the greatest power.

Their lightsaber is added to the memorial vault, and their name etched into the chronicles of Dromund Kaas.

The Holocron Oath

Before a holocron containing the knowledge of the ancients, the Sith swears the Holocron Oath: to protect the secrets of the Order, to expand the Empire's dominion, and to never bow to weakness.
